Computer Fails to Start

That can happen all of a sudden without any obvious trigger. Your system often starts loading but stops responding during startup phase after some time. Many factors can cause the problem.

Computer Freezes and Restarts

There may be two kinds of problems causing the computer to freeze and restart. First and most likely one is a software (Window or Mac) issue. Viruses, trojans, rogues and other adware may cause instability on the network. Once systems detect an issue it restarts to prevent potential damage.Second and most serious – hardware problem . Computer overheating is the most likely cause of computer freezing and restarting. Our engineer fixed this problem at your doorstep.

Noisy Computer

Fans and the hard drive are the two main causes with excess noise on computers. Furthermore, hard disks will create noise when the platters spin and the head searches for data. Loud noise is typically a really dangerous warning, and should be handled instantly.
